India in talks to sell supersonic BrahMos missile to UAE, sources say



India is in talks to sell its supersonic BrahMos missile to the United Arab Emirates (UAE), according to sources familiar with the matter. The discussions are part of India's efforts to expand its defense exports and strengthen strategic ties with Gulf countries. The BrahMos missile, developed jointly by India and Russia, is known for its speed and precision, making it a sought-after weapon system in the global arms market. The potential sale to the UAE would mark a significant milestone in India's defense export ambitions and could enhance the UAE's military capabilities. The negotiations are ongoing, and both sides are expected to work towards finalizing the deal in the near future.
