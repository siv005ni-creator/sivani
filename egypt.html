Egypt register first-ever FIFA World Cup victory, beat New Zealand 3-1



Egypt achieved a historic milestone by securing their first-ever victory in the FIFA World Cup, defeating New Zealand with a score of 3-1. The match showcased Egypt's determination and skill, as they overcame their opponents to claim this significant win. This victory not only marks a proud moment for Egyptian football but also highlights the team's growth and potential on the international stage. Fans and supporters celebrated this achievement, recognizing it as a testament to the hard work and dedication of the players and coaching staff.
